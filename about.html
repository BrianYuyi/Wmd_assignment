Our Story

Faith-inspired apparel that reflects God's image in us

Our Mission

To create high-quality, faith-inspired clothing that helps believers express their faith boldly and encourages others to seek Christ.

Our Vision

To see a generation clothed in garments that reflect God's image, sparking conversations about faith wherever they go.

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2020, His Image Apparel began as a small ministry founded by Mr Caleb Moe and named it "Kids Living In Christ Are Cool Clothing". Merchandise was distributed door-to-door and meet-ups at designated delivery points.

As he continued to embark on his mission to spread the gospel efficiently amongst the Youth, He met and collaborated with Brian Yuyi, establishing a partnership and renaming the ministry into the contemporary "His Image Apparel".

Today the ministry is one of the top-3 BESTSELLERS in the clothing market and works hand-in-hand with companies such as The BIG MONGOLIE. This success comes from the positive influence it brought amongst the "Now Generation".

Our Journey

2022
